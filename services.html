Our Services



Listed below are just a few of the many services that Marc Balch Construction provides to its clients. If there is anything that you see that is not listed, please head over to the contact page, and send us an email with the specific request. We would be happy to see if we are able to accommodate your request.

+
Custom Homes

We will build you a custom home to your specifications. You simply tell us what you would like built, we provide with an estimate, and in time you will have a beautiful new home!

+
Cabinetry

We have many years of experience in fine woodworking. Marc Balch Construction will build you completely custom cabinetry to compliment the rest of your home.

+
Remodeling

If you are a current homeowner looking to upgrade your home in any way, we would be happy to provide those remodeling services for you.

+
Flooring

The style of flooring that you put in your house is essential. Hardwood flooring is especially unique, and gives not only durability, but character to your home.

+
Roofing

Roofs only last so long, especially through winter months. If your roof begins to leak or you would like to change the appearance, we will be able to remove the existing roof and provide you with a new one.

+
Decks

Decks are a great way to add value to your home. They significantly enhance the look of your home, and allow for things such as hot tubs, grills, patio seating, etc.
