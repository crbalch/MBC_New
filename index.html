Welcome!



You have reached Marc Balch Construction, Custom Home Builders serving the Southern Vermont area, including Mt. Snow and Stratton, for over 25 years. We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality materials and service, at competitive prices. Please take a look around the site to see what services we provide, as well as our portfolio, where you can view photos of many projects. Feel free to give us a call, or send us an email for an estimate on any of the services that we provide.

Plan

We work with you from the beginning to determine your requirements up front. We help plan exactly what you would like, so we can provide you the most accurate estimate, as well as the highest quality work.

Design

In addtion to planning out requirements, we are able to help you determine materials, appliances, finishes, etc. in order for you to get the exact look and feel you are going for.

Build

We pride ourselves on completing the project on schedule, and within budget. This has always been our priority, all without cutting any corners.
